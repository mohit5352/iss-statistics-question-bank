(a) \(-\frac{2}{25}f'(t)\) for some \(t \in [0, 2]\)

(c) \(-\frac{1}{300}f''(t)\) for some \(t \in [0, 2]\)
(d) \(-\frac{1}{150}f'''(t)\) for some \(t \in [0, 2]\)
