1. [Topic: Probability of Union of Independent Events]
Let \( A_1, A_2, A_3 \) and \( A_4 \) be independent events such that \( P(A_1) = \frac{1}{2}, P(A_2) = \frac{1}{4}, P(A_3) = \frac{1}{8} \) and \( P(A_4) = \frac{1}{16} \). What is \( P(A_1 \cup A_2 \cup A_3 \cup A_4) \) equal to?
(a) \( \frac{15}{16} \)
(b) \( \frac{1023}{1024} \)
(c) \( \frac{709}{1024} \)
(d) \( \frac{315}{1024} \)
