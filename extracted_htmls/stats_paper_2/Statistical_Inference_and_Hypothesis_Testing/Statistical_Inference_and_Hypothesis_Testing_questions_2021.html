1. \( \sum x_i^2 \) is sufficient for \( \mu \).
2. MLE of \( \mu \) is \( \frac{-1 + \sqrt{1 + 4m_2}}{2} \), where \( m_2 = \frac{\sum x_i^2}{n} \).
Which of the above statements is/are correct?
(a) 1 only
(b) 2 only
(c) Both 1 and 2
(d) Neither 1 nor 2
